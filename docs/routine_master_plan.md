# The Hybrid Athlete "Life Plan": Detailed Execution Guide

## Client Profile
*   **Age/Gender:** 24M
*   **Height:** 5'7"
*   **Background:** 5y CrossFit, 5y Bodybuilding
*   **Current Maxes used for Calculation:**
    *   Squat: 345 lbs
    *   Deadlift: 386 lbs
    *   Bench: 245 lbs
    *   1 Mile Run: 7:45
*   **Heart Rate Zones:**
    *   **Zone 2 (Aerobic Base):** 146–160 bpm (STRICT)
    *   **Zone 4 (Threshold):** 175–185 bpm
    *   **Zone 5 (Max):** 190+ bpm

---

## PHASE 1: AEROBIC BASE & STRUCTURAL INTEGRITY (Weeks 1–8)
**Goal:** Expand blood volume, tendon stiffness, and movement efficiency.
**The Grind:** High volume, low intensity. Do not exceed prescribed percentages.

### Phase 1 Weekly Template (Weeks 1–4)

#### MONDAY: Lower Body Hypertrophy & Flush
*   **Warm-up (10 min):**
    *   5 min Air Bike (Easy)
    *   3 Rounds: 10 Air Squats, 10 Cossack Squats, 10 Glute Bridges.
*   **Main Lift: Low Bar Back Squat**
    *   Set 1: 135 lbs x 10 (Warmup)
    *   Set 2: 185 lbs x 5 (Warmup)
    *   **Work Sets:** 3 sets x 8 reps @ 240 lbs (70% 1RM).
    *   **Rest:** 3 minutes strictly.
*   **Accessory A: Dumbbell Walking Lunges**
    *   3 sets x 12 steps per leg.
    *   Weight: 35 lb dumbbells in each hand.
*   **Accessory B: Leg Extensions (Machine)**
    *   3 sets x 15 reps @ RPE 7 (Burn, not failure).
*   **Cardio Flush:**
    *   Apparatus: Bike Erg.
    *   Time: 15:00 minutes.
    *   Intensity: Constant RPM. HR Zone 1 (130-140 bpm). Do not sprint.

#### TUESDAY: Upper Push/Pull & Row Volume
*   **Warm-up (10 min):** Band Pull-aparts, Arm Circles, Push-ups.
*   **Main Lift: Bench Press**
    *   Set 1: 135 lbs x 10
    *   Set 2: 155 lbs x 5
    *   **Work Sets:** 3 sets x 8 reps @ 170 lbs (70% 1RM).
    *   **Rest:** 2-3 minutes.
*   **Antagonist Lift: Pendlay Row**
    *   **Work Sets:** 3 sets x 10 reps @ 135 lbs (Strict form, back parallel to floor).
*   **Accessory Supersets (3 Rounds):**
    *   Dumbbell Overhead Press: 10 reps (40 lbs).
    *   Face Pulls: 15 reps (Cable).
*   **Cardio: Row Erg Intervals (Aerobic Power)**
    *   Warm-up: 2 min easy row.
    *   Workout: 5 x 500m.
    *   Pace Target: 2:15/500m pace.
    *   Rest: 1:00 minute rest between intervals.
    *   **Constraint:** HR must recover below 140 bpm before starting next interval.

#### WEDNESDAY: Pure Zone 2 Endurance
*   **Activity:** Outdoor Run (Weather permitting) or Treadmill (1% Incline).
*   **Duration (Week 1):** 35 Minutes.
*   **Duration (Week 2):** 40 Minutes.
*   **Duration (Week 3):** 45 Minutes.
*   **Duration (Week 4):** 30 Minutes (Deload).
*   **Pace:** Variable. You run as slow as needed to keep HR between 146–160 bpm.
*   **Constraint:** If HR hits 162, you walk until it hits 145, then jog again. DO NOT compromise this rule.

#### THURSDAY: Athleticism & Flow (The "CrossFit" Day)
*   **Warm-up:** Jump Rope (3 mins).
*   **Skill:** Double Unders.
    *   Protocol: EMOM (Every Minute on the Minute) for 10 minutes.
    *   Work: 30 seconds of practice. 30 seconds rest.
*   **Power: Power Clean**
    *   Work Sets: 5 sets x 3 reps @ 135 lbs.
    *   Focus: Perfect technique, fast elbows. Not heavy.
*   **MetCon: "Aerobic Flow"**
    *   Format: 20 Minute AMRAP.
    *   Intensity: 70% Effort. Nasal breathing only.
    *   The Work:
        *   200m Run
        *   10 Toes-to-Bar (Strict or small kip)
        *   15 Kettlebell Swings (53 lbs / 24kg)
    *   **Note:** You should finish this workout feeling energized, not destroyed.

#### FRIDAY: Full Body Hypertrophy (Pump)
*   **Superset A (4 Sets):**
    *   Dumbbell Incline Bench Press: 10-12 reps (55 lbs).
    *   Weighted Pull-ups: 5-8 reps (Bodyweight or +10lbs).
*   **Superset B (3 Sets):**
    *   Romanian Deadlift (Barbell): 10 reps @ 185 lbs.
    *   Dumbbell Lateral Raises: 15 reps (20 lbs).
*   **The "Beach" Finisher (3 Sets):**
    *   EZ Bar Bicep Curls: 12 reps.
    *   Tricep Rope Pushdowns: 15 reps.
    *   Hanging Leg Raise: 15 reps.

#### SATURDAY: The Long Aerobic Session
*   **The Setup:** Gym access required (access to multiple machines).
*   **Format:** 45 Minutes Continuous Movement.
*   **The Rotation:**
    *   0:00 – 10:00: Row Erg (Pace: 2:20/500m).
    *   10:00 – 20:00: Bike Erg (Zone 2 RPM).
    *   20:00 – 30:00: Ski Erg or Elliptical.
    *   30:00 – 40:00: Run (Treadmill or Loop outside).
    *   40:00 – 45:00: Row Erg cooldown.
*   **Target HR:** Average 150 bpm.

#### SUNDAY: Recovery
*   **Mandatory:** 30 mins Yoga flow or Static Stretching.
*   **Prohibited:** Lifting weights or running.

### Phase 1 Progression Logic (Weeks 5–8)
*   **Weeks 5-7:** Increase Squat/Bench weight by 5-10 lbs per week. Increase Wednesday Run by 5 mins per week.
*   **Week 8 (Deload):** Reduce all weights to 50% 1RM. Reduce Run to 30 mins.

## PHASE 2: STRENGTH & THRESHOLD (Weeks 9–20)
**Goal:** Force production and Lactic Tolerance.
**The Grind:** Heavier weights, uncomfortable cardio.

### Phase 2 Weekly Template (Weeks 9–12)

#### MONDAY: Heavy Lower
*   **Warm-up:** Box Jumps (3 sets of 5, moderate height).
*   **Main Lift: Back Squat**
    *   Work Sets: 5 sets x 3 reps @ 295 lbs (85% 1RM).
    *   Rest: 3-5 minutes.
*   **Secondary Lift: Deadlift**
    *   Work Sets: 3 sets x 3 reps @ 325 lbs (85% 1RM).
    *   Note: Reset fully after every rep. No bouncing.
*   **Accessory:**
    *   Bulgarian Split Squats: 3 x 8/leg (holding 40lb DBs).

#### TUESDAY: Threshold Intervals
*   **Activity:** Running (Track or Treadmill preferred for pacing).
*   **Warm-up:** 10 min easy jog + Drills (High knees, butt kicks).
*   **The Workout:** 3 x 8 Minutes.
    *   Pace Target: 8:45 – 9:00 / mile pace. (This is slightly slower than your 1 mile PR, sustained).
    *   Rest: 2 minutes walking rest between intervals.
*   **Cool Down:** 5 min walk.

#### WEDNESDAY: Heavy Upper
*   **Main Lift: Strict Overhead Press (Standing)**
    *   Work Sets: 5 sets x 5 reps @ 95-105 lbs (Start conservative).
*   **Secondary Lift: Weighted Pull-ups**
    *   Work Sets: 5 sets x 3 reps (Bodyweight + 25 lbs).
*   **Accessory:**
    *   Dips (Weighted): 3 sets x 10 reps.
    *   Single Arm DB Row: 3 sets x 10 reps (Heavy, 70lbs+).

#### THURSDAY: Dynamic Effort & MetCon
*   **Power: Power Snatch**
    *   Work Sets: 6 sets x 2 reps @ 95 lbs.
    *   Focus: Speed. Rest 45 seconds between sets.
*   **MetCon: "Lactic Bath"**
    *   Format: 5 Rounds for Time.
    *   Intensity: 85-90% Effort.
    *   Cap: 15 Minutes.
    *   The Work:
        *   15 Wall Balls (20lb)
        *   12 Box Jumps (24")
        *   9 Burpees over Box

#### FRIDAY: Prehab / Accessory
*   **Circuit (4 Rounds, not for time):**
    *   Single Leg RDL (KB): 10/leg.
    *   Copenhagen Plank: 30 sec/side.
    *   Z-Press (Seated on floor): 10 reps (light DBs).
    *   Pallof Press: 12 reps/side.

#### SATURDAY: Tempo Run
*   **Distance:** 4 Miles Total.
*   **Splits:**
    *   Mile 1: Easy Warmup (10:00 pace).
    *   Mile 2 & 3: TEMPO @ 8:15 – 8:30 pace. (Uncomfortable but sustainable).
    *   Mile 4: Easy Cooldown.

#### SUNDAY: Rest

### Phase 2 Progression Logic
*   **Weeks 13-16:** Increase Squat to 305 lbs (5x3). Increase Threshold Intervals to 3 x 10 mins.
*   **Week 14:** Deload (Light weights, easy cardio).
*   **Weeks 17-19:** Increase Squat to 315 lbs (3x3). Increase Tempo Run to 3 miles at pace.

## PHASE 3: THE PEAK (Weeks 21–32)
**Goal:** Peak Power, Speed, and PRs.
**The Grind:** Short duration, Maximum Intensity.

### Phase 3 Weekly Template
*   **MONDAY: Max Effort Lower + Alactic Power**
    *   Main Lift: Squat
        *   Build to a Heavy Double (2 reps).
        *   Target: 315 lbs -> 335 lbs -> 350 lbs (PR attempt in Week 30).
    *   Sprint Work: Assault Bike.
        *   Protocol: 10 Rounds.
        *   Work: 20 seconds MAX EFFORT (100% Watts).
        *   Rest: 1:40 min slow pedal.
        *   Note: This will hurt.

*   **TUESDAY: Track Speed (VO2 Max)**
    *   Location: 400m Track.
    *   Warm-up: 1 mile jog + extensive dynamic stretching.
    *   The Workout: 400m Repeats.
    *   Reps: 8.
    *   Target Pace: 1:50 – 1:55 per lap (7:20-7:40 mile pace).
    *   Rest: 2:00 minutes (1:1 work:rest ratio).

*   **WEDNESDAY: Max Effort Upper + CrossFit Benchmark**
    *   Main Lift: Bench Press
        *   Build to a Heavy Single.
        *   Target: 225 lbs -> 235 lbs -> 250 lbs (PR attempt).
    *   MetCon: "Fran" (or variation)
        *   21-15-9 reps of: Thrusters (95 lbs), Pull-ups.
        *   Goal: Sub 5 minutes.

*   **THURSDAY: Active Flush (Zone 1/2)**
    *   Activity: Swim or Easy Spin.
    *   Time: 45 minutes.
    *   Intensity: Very low. Recovery is critical for Friday.

*   **FRIDAY: Olympic Lifting & Plyos**
    *   Lift: Clean & Jerk
        *   EMOM for 10 minutes: 1 rep @ 185 lbs.
    *   Plyos:
        *   Depth Jumps (off 12" box): 4 sets x 5 reps.
        *   Broad Jumps: 4 sets x 3 reps (Max distance).

*   **SATURDAY: Simulation Day (Game Day)**
    *   Week 21-24: 5k Run for Time (Race Effort).
    *   Week 25-28: "Murph" (Partitioned: 5-10-15).
    *   Week 29-32: 1 Mile Run Time Trial (PR Attempt).

*   **SUNDAY: Total Rest**

---

## PHASE 4: THE WASHOUT (Weeks 33–36)
**Goal:** Drop fatigue.
*   **Weights:** Dumbbells only. Nothing heavier than 50lbs.
*   **Cardio:** Nature walks/hikes only. No timers.
*   **Frequency:** Train when you feel like it (3-4 days).

---

## PHASE 5: RE-CALIBRATION (Weeks 37–52)
*   **Action:** Re-test 1RM Squat, Deadlift, Bench, and 1 Mile Run.
*   **Restart Phase 1:** Use new numbers.
*   **Expectation:** Your Zone 2 pace should now be 9:30/mile instead of 10:30+, and your Phase 1 Squat weights should be 265 lbs instead of 240.
